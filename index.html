Tip: Keep digital copies of completion emails/certificates (e.g., CITI completion, library training). You’ll upload them when submitting documentation.

Required activities

Complete all items below.
  1. Attend one meeting of the student Anthropology Club.
  2. Attend and do reflection brief for six qualifying department events.
  3. Attend one Capstone Seminar Symposium prior to your senior year (prior to the year you present your own capstone research project).
  4. Complete a bibliographic research training session.
  5. Complete research ethics training module (CITI).
  6. Professional development activity 1: Career Resource Center workshop on CV/resume preparation.
  7. Professional development activity 2: Professional academic organization explorations.
